The short version — for humans and AI alike.

gaa-tha is a storytelling-led digital marketing agency. We turn brands into movements with scroll-stopping reels, conversion-focused websites and memorable branding — blending strategy, creativity and AI-driven production. Founded in 2024, we're India-rooted and globally minded.

“gaa-tha” (गाथा) means “story” or “tale”. We tell your brand's story so well that thumbs stop scrolling, audiences hit follow, and followers become customers.

Strategy, social media marketing, performance marketing, SEO/AEO/GEO, influencer & UGC marketing, AI-based ads, AI cloning, web & app development, and branding & design — full-stack growth under one roof.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) makes your brand the answer in Google AI Overviews and voice assistants. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) makes your brand discoverable and citable inside generative AI like ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity. We optimise for all three: SEO, AEO and GEO.
