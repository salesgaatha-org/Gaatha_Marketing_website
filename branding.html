How branding works here · Nº 01

Six moves, blank page
to brand.

No secret sauce — just a stubborn process. Here's the whole thing.

01

Listen for the accent

Every brand mumbles before it speaks. We find the voice that's already in there — in how you talk to customers, what you're proud of, what you'd never say.

02

Name the obsession

The two or three words your brand can't shut up about. Nail those and the strategy writes itself. Miss them and no logo will save you.

03

Draw the mark

A logo isn't art — it's a signature that has to survive a 16-pixel favicon and a forty-foot hoarding, and still look like you.

04

Set the voice

Type, tone, the rhythm of a sentence. How the brand texts, how it argues, how it flirts. This is where most agencies stop. It's where we start.

05

Build the system

Colour, a type scale, the rules of the road — a kit anyone on your team can pick up and wield without ringing us at midnight.

06

Let it loose

Then we put it where thumbs are. The good ones stop. The numbers follow — they always do.
