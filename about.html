Our Story

Between late-night brainstorming and global campaign deep-dives, we cracked the formula: emotion + storytelling + social media. We built gaa-tha to turn brand moments into movements — mixing taste, rigour and speed.

Brands craved engagement and loyalty but were lost in the digital noise. Algorithms favoured quantity over quality, and audiences scrolled past forgettable creative.
